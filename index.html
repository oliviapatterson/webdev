Background

I learned how to do spray tans my senior year of high school.

My best friend, Bailey's, mom had a spray tan machine. Bailey would give me spray tans, but I could never do hers. Bailey ended up teaching me how to do it and I was abel to get a lot of practice doing each other.

Business Idea

I took an entrepreneurship class my second year of BYU. This class inspired me to start this business. I knew that self-tanning was very popular in Utah, so I took the idea and ran with it.

First Machine

When moving to Provo I decided to invest in a spray tan machine. I researched and got reccomendations and made the purchase. I have loved being able to grow my clientelle and I'm loving meeting all of you!
